Our Story

Building Leaders Since 2005

Good Shepherd Academy was established in 2005 with a vision to create a world-class educational institution that nurtures innovation, critical thinking, and character development.

Over the past two decades, we have grown from a small learning center to a premier educational institution with 2000+ students, 150+ expert faculty members, and 5 premium industry-aligned programs.
